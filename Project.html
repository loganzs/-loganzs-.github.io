Bill Gates' Finances

How he made his billions

Bill Gates was a successfull businessman who dropped out of college at harvard university to start his company "Microsoft". Microsoft became incredibly succesful as the worlds largest PC software company. Bill Gates had positions within the company as CEO, Chairman and Cheif Software Architect as well as being the majority shareholder within the company.

Philanthropy

He is also a generous philanthropist. His organization, The Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, is the wealthiest charity organization in the world.

Family Life
